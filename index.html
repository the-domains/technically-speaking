How will anybody know how your components should look if you don't ASCII render them in the code?

My mind was set in gear by this. If we believe that the code base can never be fully understood by all employees simultaneously, then it makes sense to incorporate shortcuts throughout.

In my experience, paper or whiteboard drawings are made and remade time and again. But rarely preserved… if we have a good process of check-in and visualisation, I'm sure it could aid strong documentation rapidly.

It seems so cool, and it makes so much sense… but actually finding a practical way to turn the casual drawings people use on white boards in code…

It doesn't work naturally.
