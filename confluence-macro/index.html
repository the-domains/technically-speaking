Expanding Confluence with Authorship

Something came up at work today that I think bears repeating. Add a customisable “author“ to Confluence pages. You may be surprised to hear it doesn’t exist.

(read on to see how its different to the existing Owner, modifier etc.)

It ticked a few boxes:

  • Sounds interesting
  • Instant, simple benefit
  • Totally feasible

But its also a “nice to have“. And that’s classically shorthand for not getting in the book of work, and not happening this year.

Then the conversation took a pleasant turn:

I’d fund someone to get this public domain…

git repo

I won’t quote the amount, but it was pretty generous, and it made me realise both how much the team wanted such a feature, and how useful it could be beyond. Shame it took a dollar sign to do that, but it was a strong enough signal to encourage me do it in my free time.
